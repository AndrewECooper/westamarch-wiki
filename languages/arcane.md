# Arcane (The Language of Magic)

## Grammar Notes

### Suffix System

**-TA** appears to be a suffix meaning "it" (possibly an object marker)

Example: **ZAN-TA** = "Suppress it"

### Word Order

Based on the Warren Anchor Stone inscription, word order appears to be:

**Verb - Object - Verb - Prepositional Phrase**

Example breakdown of **ZAN-TA KYDRA TESS AQUA MAGI**:
- **ZAN-TA** (Verb-Object) = Suppress it
- **KYDRA** (Subject/Object) = Stone/Anchor
- **TESS** (Verb) = Channel
- **AQUA MAGI** (Prepositional phrase) = to the Water of Vision

### Compound Words

Some concepts are expressed through hyphenated compounds:

- **TESS-KYDRA** = Channel-Anchor (the ritual crucible)
- **AQUA MAGI** = Water of Vision (likely referring to [the Singing Pools](/locations/thornwood/singing-pools))

## The Warren Anchor Stone Inscription

The complete inscription found on the South Anchor Stone in [The Warrens](/locations/westmarch/the-warrens):

> **ZAN-TA KYDRA TESS AQUA MAGI**

Translation:
> *"Suppress it, Stone, Channel to the Water of Vision"*

This inscription reveals the Anchor Stone's function: it suppresses (ZAN-TA) through the stone (KYDRA) by channeling (TESS) power to the Water of Vision (AQUA MAGI)—likely [the Singing Pools](/locations/thornwood/singing-pools), the Greenheart's primary sensorium.
