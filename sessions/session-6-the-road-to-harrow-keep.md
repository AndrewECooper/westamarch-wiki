# Session 6: The Road to Harrow Keep

**Date:** Greenleaf 30th, 1247

## New Companions

As the party gathered to set off for [Harrow Keep](/locations/thornwood/harrow-keep), [Wynn Copper](/characters/npcs/wynn-copper) arrived with a friend in tow: [Thalia](/characters/player-characters/thalia), a halfling rogue and former city watch investigator. The group also found themselves unexpectedly joined by [Drakov](/characters/player-characters/drakov), a massive red Dragonborn cleric of Bahamut, sent by his temple's high priest to serve as a bodyguard for [Lady Sera](/characters/npcs/lady-sera)—the Duke's daughter.

## Journey into the Thornwood

The party organized themselves into travel roles for the trek through [the Thornwood](/locations/thornwood). [Kaelen](/characters/player-characters/kaelen-vale) put his ranger skills to excellent use, hunting down a deer during the day's march. [Wynn](/characters/npcs/wynn-copper) proved a surprisingly capable cook, turning the kill into a hearty stew for the group. Drakov, tasked with prayers for the journey, botched his duties spectacularly.

## The Ogre

The next morning, Thalia scouted ahead and spotted a massive ogre stomping out of the heavy forest. The creature sized up the party and grunted, "Looks good. Breakfast."

[Quill](/characters/player-characters/quill) opened with a *Firebolt*, and [Wynn](/characters/npcs/wynn-copper) charged in with a staggering blow that knocked the ogre prone. Thalia struck from the shadows with her shortbow, landing a sneak attack, while [Kaelen](/characters/player-characters/kaelen-vale) followed up with an *Arcing Blow* from his lightning-wreathed scimitar.

The ogre rose furious and swept its greatclub in a devastating arc, dropping [Sera](/characters/npcs/lady-sera) unconscious and severely injuring Drakov. It was [Quill](/characters/player-characters/quill) who turned the tide—enraged by [Sera](/characters/npcs/lady-sera) falling, the fairy wizard unleashed a second-level *Shattering Barrage*, summoning orbs of molten glass that shredded the ogre. [Wynn](/characters/npcs/wynn-copper) stepped in to land the killing blow with his longsword.

[Quill](/characters/player-characters/quill) rushed to heal [Sera](/characters/npcs/lady-sera), and the party looted 42 gold, a Potion of Diminution, and a Potion of Resistance (Poison), from the ogre's belt before taking a short rest.

## The Kobold Ambush

Continuing deeper into the forest, Thalia's keen perception spotted a group of kobolds lying in ambush along the path. [Quill](/characters/player-characters/quill) reacted first, casting a second-level *Sleep* that immediately neutralized three of the ambushers. [Kaelen](/characters/player-characters/kaelen-vale) followed with *Entangle*, and erupting vines restrained the remaining conscious enemies.

The kobolds fought back fiercely despite their disadvantage. One fired a poison blow dart, another cast *Magic Missile*, and a third shattered a vial of poison gas that engulfed [Sera](/characters/npcs/lady-sera) and [Quill](/characters/player-characters/quill). The party whittled them down with arcane bolts and arrows until the last conscious kobold dropped his weapon and surrendered, groveling before the massive Dragonborn and calling Drakov "great master."

## Prisoners

Rather than executing the survivors, Drakov and [Wynn](/characters/npcs/wynn-copper) tied up the kobolds for questioning. The party made camp with their prisoners as the forest darkened around them—[Harrow Keep](/locations/thornwood/harrow-keep) close at last.
