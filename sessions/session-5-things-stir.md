# Session 5: Things Stir

**Date:** Greenleaf 28th, 1247

## Preparations

The party spent the afternoon gathering supplies for the journey to [Harrow Keep](/locations/thornwood/harrow-keep). [Kaelen](/characters/player-characters/kaelen-vale) confirmed he could hunt and forage along the way, but the GM warned them: the deep forest held monstrous humanoids, bears, and drakes.

Before departing, the party reviewed what they knew. The Tess-Kydra was last held at [Harrow Keep](/locations/thornwood/harrow-keep), captured when the keep fell during [the Green War](/history/the-green-war). The kobolds of the Poison Scale—servants of the green dragon **Verdanthis**—were believed to still lair in the ruins, though the dragon itself hadn't been seen in hundreds of years.

They also discussed [Lady Sera's](/characters/npcs/lady-sera) arrangement: an audience with [the Duke](/characters/npcs/duke-aldric) in exchange for accompanying them. The party remained suspicious of [Lord Brennan](/characters/npcs/lord-brennan) and his involvement in the city's troubles. [Sera](/characters/npcs/lady-sera) had mentioned that the Duke wears a mysterious vial of liquid around his neck—something meant to be passed down to her eventually.

## Attack at the Market

[Kaelen](/characters/player-characters/kaelen-vale) led [Sera](/characters/npcs/lady-sera) and [Lyrique](/characters/player-characters/lyrique) to a farmers market in a cul-de-sac in [the Greenmark](/locations/westmarch/the-greenmark). Without warning, terrified townspeople came running past, screaming about things coming out of the nearby warded park. Small animated shrub creatures scurried out of the mist and began destroying market stalls.

[Kaelen](/characters/player-characters/kaelen-vale) drew his scimitar and shield, ordering the others to stay behind him. [Lyrique](/characters/player-characters/lyrique) sang a battle hymn, granting her allies temporary hit points.

[Kaelen](/characters/player-characters/kaelen-vale) struck first, casting *Arcing Blow*—his scimitar cut a shrub in half with slashing and lightning damage. [Lyrique](/characters/player-characters/lyrique) fired her hand crossbow but barely scratched the creature; it retaliated by clawing at her and tearing her dress. [Sera](/characters/npcs/lady-sera) hung back and fired an arcane bolt that blew one of the shrubs to pieces.

## Enter Wynn Copper

An armored man came charging into the alley with sword and shield. He paused mid-fight to tell [Lyrique](/characters/player-characters/lyrique) she was pretty, then smashed a shrub to splinters.

Moments later, a massive creature resembling a walking tree stomped out of the park. [Kaelen](/characters/player-characters/kaelen-vale) focused on the large creature, marking it as his quarry and expending everything he had to deliver a devastating blow—33 points of damage. [Lyrique](/characters/player-characters/lyrique) cast *Bane* on the creature to weaken its attacks. The armored stranger adopted a defensive stance, granting [Kaelen](/characters/player-characters/kaelen-vale) an armor class bonus that caused the tree creature's attack to miss. The stranger then drove his longsword through it, reducing it to a pile of twigs and leaves.

## Aftermath

The armored man introduced himself as [Wynn Copper](/characters/npcs/wynn-copper), a guard with the [Delver's Guild](/factions/delvers-guild). [Lyrique](/characters/player-characters/lyrique) gave him her handkerchief to wipe his face. He kept it, tucking it away as a knightly favor.

As the townspeople returned, a merchant mourned his destroyed crates of cabbages and confirmed the creatures had come from the park, where the magical mist was supposed to be contained by wards.

[Sera](/characters/npcs/lady-sera) examined the ward with her arcane senses. The ward was still active, but she couldn't tell if its magic had weakened. The party theorized that motes of light from the mist were escaping and animating local plant life—the same phenomenon they had witnessed in [the Warrens](/locations/westmarch/the-warrens) during [the Gray Morning](/sessions/session-1-the-gray-morning).

## A New Companion

[Lyrique](/characters/player-characters/lyrique) asked [Wynn](/characters/npcs/wynn-copper) if he was available for hire and invited him to join them on the journey to [Harrow Keep](/locations/thornwood/harrow-keep). Despite learning the trip would take several days and that they were heading toward a potential dragon, [Wynn](/characters/npcs/wynn-copper) enthusiastically agreed to come along and protect them.

## Kaelen's Knowledge

Before setting out, [Kaelen](/characters/player-characters/kaelen-vale) drew on his knowledge of kobolds. While individually weak, they were prideful, clever, and highly dangerous in numbers. They swarmed, used specialized magic users and alchemists, and should not be underestimated.
