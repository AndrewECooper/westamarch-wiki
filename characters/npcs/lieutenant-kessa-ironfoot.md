# Lieutenant Kessa Ironfoot

## Role
Second-in-command of the [City Watch](/factions/city-watch)

## Race and Age
Mountain Dwarf, 89 years old

## Location
The Watchtower, Oldtown

## Description
Handles the Watch's relationship with the temples and military matters. Strict but fair, she's a devout follower of Bahamut and runs things by the book.

## What She Knows
**[Tier 1-2 knowledge](/lore/knowledge-tiers)**: Standard knowledge of [the Greenwood Pact](/lore/the-greenwood-pact) and Westmarch's history. Nothing special beyond what most educated citizens know.

## Current State
Serving as second-in-command to [Captain Garrett Stone](/characters/npcs/captain-garrett-stone), managing the Watch's day-to-day operations with particular focus on coordination with religious institutions and military discipline.

## Motivation
Uphold the law, serve justice, honor Bahamut.

## Connections and Relationships
- **Faction**: Second-in-command of the [City Watch](/factions/city-watch)
- **Superior**: [Captain Garrett Stone](/characters/npcs/captain-garrett-stone)
- **Faith**: Devout follower of Bahamut
- **Temple Liaison**: Primary contact between the Watch and [the Temples](/factions/the-temples), particularly [the Platinum Basilica](/locations/westmarch/platinum-basilica)
- **Coordinates with**: [High Priest Davian Goldscale](/characters/npcs/davian-goldscale)

## Leadership Philosophy

## Temple Relations
As the Watch's primary liaison to the temples, Kessa manages the complex relationship between secular law enforcement and religious institutions. She coordinates on matters of sanctuary law, temple security, and cases involving religious disputes.

## By the Book
Kessa's insistence on proper procedure sometimes frustrates those who want quick results, but her approach has prevented numerous legal complications and maintains the Watch's reputation for fairness. In a city where noble politics often interfere with justice, her unwavering adherence to protocol provides stability.
