# Elder Marissa Chen

**Role:** Leader of the Wayfarers' Hall refugee community
**Race/Age:** Human Cleric of Avandra, 80
**Location:** [The Wayfarers' Hall](/locations/westmarch/wayfarers-hall), [the Warrens](/locations/westmarch/the-warrens)

## Description

Pragmatic and compassionate, Elder Chen leads the refugee community that has settled in the Warrens. Despite her advanced age, she remains a tireless advocate for her people, working to help them build new lives in Westmarch.

She was injured in the attack during the emergence of the Fog Beast from the Undercroft beneath the Wayfarers' Hall, but has since recovered. The attack left her shaken but no less determined to protect her community.

## What She Knows

**Tier 1 knowledge.** Elder Chen knows something terrible came from beneath the Hall and killed people during the attack. She doesn't understand the supernatural cause or the deeper mysteries at work, but she knows her community is in danger.

## Current State

Recovered from her injuries but deeply concerned about the safety of her community. She continues to lead the refugee settlement while remaining vigilant for any further threats from below.

## Motivation

- Protect her community at all costs
- Help refugees build new lives in Westmarch
- Ensure the survival and prosperity of those who depend on her
- Find answers about what attacked them from beneath the Hall

## Connections

- Leads the refugee community in [the Warrens](/locations/westmarch/the-warrens)
- Works closely with the [Wayfarers' Hall](/locations/westmarch/wayfarers-hall) as a place of worship and community gathering
- Connected to the temples in the Temple Quarter through her role as a cleric of Avandra
