# Wynn Copper

## Role
Mercenary and member of the [Delver's Guild](/factions/delvers-guild)

## Race and Age
Human, late 20s

## Location
[The Greenmark](/locations/westmarch/the-greenmark)

## Description
A broad-shouldered, heavily armored man-at-arms with a near-permanent grin and an inexhaustible supply of optimism. Wynn is relentlessly cheerful, earnestly encouraging, and almost annoyingly positive—the kind of person who responds to a cave-in with "Well, at least we know where the ceiling is now." He is, however, genuinely competent. He knows how to hold a line, protect his allies, and keep people alive in bad situations.

He is thoroughly smitten with [Lyrique](/characters/player-characters/lyrique) and has appointed himself her personal protector, whether she asked for one or not.

## Personality
- Unfailingly cheerful, even in dire circumstances
- Encouraging to a fault—always sees the best in people and situations
- Loyal and protective, especially of those he cares about
- Not stupid, just optimistic; reads situations well even if his response is a smile
- Can be grating to cynics and pessimists, but his sincerity is hard to resent for long

## Motivation
Keep people safe. Get paid. Follow [Lyrique](/characters/player-characters/lyrique) around and make sure she doesn't die.

## Connections and Relationships
- **Faction**: [Delver's Guild](/factions/delvers-guild)
- **Guildmaster**: [Torven Blackoak](/characters/npcs/torven-blackoak)
- **Infatuation**: [Lyrique](/characters/player-characters/lyrique)

**Saving Throws** STR +5, CON +5
**Skills** Athletics +5, Perception +3, Intimidation +2
**Languages** Common

**Longsword.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 7 (1d8 + 3) slashing damage.

### Special Abilities

**Hold the Line (At Will).** As a bonus action, Wynn adopts a defensive stance. Until the start of his next turn, allies within 5 feet of him gain +2 AC, and any hostile creature that moves within his reach provokes an opportunity attack.

**Staggering Blow (Recharge 4–6).** When Wynn hits with a melee attack, the target must make a DC 13 STR save or be knocked prone and have its speed reduced to 0 until the end of its next turn.
