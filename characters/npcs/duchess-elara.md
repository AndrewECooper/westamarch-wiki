# Duchess Elara Westmarch

## Role
Duke's wife, manages court functions

## Race and Age
Human, 48 years old

## Location
[The Citadel](/locations/westmarch/the-citadel)

## Description
Diplomatic and capable, the Duchess has taken over many day-to-day court functions as her husband withdraws. She is clearly worried about [Aldric](/characters/npcs/duke-aldric) but maintains composure in public.

## What She Knows
**Unclear**: She may have been told some of the family secrets, or Aldric may be keeping her in the dark to protect her. Her exact level of knowledge about [the Binding](/lore/the-binding) and the true nature of [the Greenwood Pact](/lore/the-greenwood-pact) is not established.

## Current State
Managing court functions and maintaining the appearance of stability while her husband grows increasingly isolated and paranoid. She bears the weight of running the court while worrying about her husband's state of mind.

## Motivation
Support her husband, protect her daughter, maintain stability at court.

## Connections and Relationships
- **Husband**: [Duke Aldric Westmarch](/characters/npcs/duke-aldric)
- **Daughter**: [Lady Sera Westmarch](/characters/npcs/lady-sera)
- **Brother-in-law**: [Lord Brennan Westmarch](/characters/npcs/lord-brennan)
- **Faction**: [House Westmarch](/factions/house-westmarch)
- **Connection**: Has arranged for her daughter Sera to study magic through connections with [Elara Moonwhisper](/characters/npcs/elara-moonwhisper) at the [Archive of Ioun](/locations/westmarch/archive-of-ioun)

## Role at Court
As Duchess, Elara has become the public face of House Westmarch's governance, managing diplomatic functions, public audiences, and day-to-day administrative decisions that her husband once handled personally.
