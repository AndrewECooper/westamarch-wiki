# Speaker Marna Goldleaf

**Role:** Elected leader of the [Merchant's Collective](/factions/merchants-collective)
**Race/Age:** Lightfoot Halfling, 67
**Location:** [The Exchange](/locations/westmarch/the-exchange), [Duke's Bridge](/locations/westmarch/dukes-bridge)

## Description

Shrewd negotiator who represents the economic interests of Westmarch's merchant class. Practical and politically savvy, Marna has built her reputation on fair dealing and hard bargaining. She understands the delicate balance between noble politics and commercial prosperity.

As an elected leader, she serves the interests of merchants, traders, innkeepers, and craftspeople throughout the city. Her decisions shape trade policy, market regulations, and the economic relationship between Westmarch and the outside world.

## What She Knows

**Tier 1-2 knowledge.** Business-focused, not particularly concerned with ancient history or supernatural mysteries. She understands trade routes, market forces, and political maneuvering far better than she understands the deeper secrets of the city.

Her knowledge is practical: what goods are moving, who's making money, where the economic pressures are building. She knows the city's prosperity depends on stability and good trade conditions.

## Motivation

- Ensure prosperity for Westmarch's merchant class
- Maintain stability that allows trade to flourish
- Secure good trade conditions and favorable policies
- Protect the economic interests of her constituents
- Balance the demands of noble politics with practical business needs

## Connections

- Leads the [Merchant's Collective](/factions/merchants-collective), an informal association representing Westmarch's economic interests
- Works from [The Exchange](/locations/westmarch/the-exchange) in [Duke's Bridge](/locations/westmarch/dukes-bridge)
- Regularly negotiates with the ducal court on matters of trade and taxation
