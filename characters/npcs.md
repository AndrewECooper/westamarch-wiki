# Non-Player Characters

The people who shape Westmarch—allies, enemies, and those whose loyalties remain unclear. This index organizes NPCs by their primary faction affiliations.

---

## House Westmarch

The ruling family and the ducal court.

- [Duke Aldric Westmarch](/characters/npcs/duke-aldric-westmarch) — Ruler of Westmarch, withdrawn and paranoid, knows the Binding is failing
- [Duchess Elara](/characters/npcs/duchess-elara) — Duke's wife and court manager, taking over public functions
- [Lord Brennan Westmarch](/characters/npcs/lord-brennan) — Duke's brother, charismatic reformer with a hidden agenda
- [Lady Sera](/characters/npcs/lady-sera) — Duke's daughter and heir, intelligent but sheltered from darker truths
- [Duke Garrick Westmarch](/characters/npcs/duke-garrick-westmarch) — Historical figure who ruled during the Binding 300 years ago

---

## The Rangers of the Green

Elite guardians of the boundary between city and forest.

- [Mira Thornweaver](/characters/npcs/mira-thornweaver) — High Ranger and commander, deeply troubled by recent events

---

## The City Watch

Law enforcement and city defense.

- [Captain Garrett Stone](/characters/npcs/captain-garrett-stone) — Watch Commander, loyal and practical
- [Lieutenant Kessa Ironfoot](/characters/npcs/lieutenant-kessa-ironfoot) — Second in command, devout follower of Bahamut

---

## The Circle of the Thornwood

Druids maintaining balance between civilization and nature.

- [Archdruid Theron Mossbark](/characters/npcs/theron-mossbark) — Ancient leader of the Circle, cryptic and withdrawn, carries centuries of guilt

---

## The Temples

Religious leaders and spiritual guides.

- [Chief Archivist Elara Moonwhisper](/characters/npcs/elara-moonwhisper) — Leader of the Archive of Ioun, has approached full understanding of the truth
- [High Priest Davian Goldscale](/characters/npcs/davian-goldscale) — Leader of the Platinum Basilica, experiencing prophetic dreams
- [Elder Marissa Chen](/characters/npcs/elder-marissa-chen) — Leader of the Wayfarers' Hall, pragmatic cleric serving refugees

---

## The Merchant's Collective

Economic leaders representing commercial interests.

- [Speaker Marna Goldleaf](/characters/npcs/marna-goldleaf) — Elected leader of the Merchant's Collective, shrewd negotiator

---

## The Delver's Guild

Adventurers and explorers venturing into dangerous places.

- [Guildmaster Torven Blackoak](/characters/npcs/torven-blackoak) — Former Ranger who now leads the Delvers, pragmatic and profit-driven

---

## The Fey Courts

Supernatural beings born from promises and oaths.

- [Lady Oona](/characters/npcs/lady-oona) — Archfey of the Winter Court, embodies Regret and Broken Promises, oversees the Singing Pools
- [Riven-Vow](/characters/npcs/riven-vow) — Redcap sentinel guarding the Singing Pools with a single riddle
- [Percival](/characters/npcs/percival) — Curious faerie dragon who observes events in the forest

---

## Other Notable Figures

Characters not primarily affiliated with major factions.

- [Archdruid Ilmaris](/characters/npcs/archdruid-ilmaris) — Historical figure who negotiated during the Greenwood Pact, possibly still alive
- [Commander Edric Harrow](/characters/npcs/commander-edric-harrow) — Historical military commander associated with Harrow Keep
