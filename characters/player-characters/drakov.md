# Drakov

**Class:** Cleric
**Heritage:** Red Dragonborn
**Deity:** Bahamut
**Level:** 3

---

## Overview

Drakov is a red Dragonborn cleric of Bahamut, sent by his temple's high priest to serve as a bodyguard for [Lady Sera](/characters/npcs/lady-sera) during the expedition to [Harrow Keep](/locations/thornwood/harrow-keep).

---

## Background

Drakov serves the temple of Bahamut in [Westmarch](/locations/westmarch). His high priest assigned him to protect [Lady Sera](/characters/npcs/lady-sera)—the Duke's daughter—on the dangerous journey into [the Thornwood](/locations/thornwood).
