## Non-Player Characters

The people who shape the world around the heroes—allies, enemies, and those whose loyalties remain unclear.

[**NPCs Index**](/characters/npcs) — Complete listing of all NPCs organized by faction

### Major Factions

- **House Westmarch** — The ruling family and those in their service
- **The Rangers of the Green** — Elite guardians of the boundary between city and forest
- **The City Watch** — Law enforcement and city defense
- **The Circle of the Thornwood** — Druids maintaining the balance between civilization and nature
- **The Temples** — Religious leaders providing spiritual guidance and practical aid
- **The Merchant's Collective** — Economic leaders representing commercial interests
- **The Delver's Guild** — Adventurers and explorers venturing into dangerous places
- **The Fey Courts** — Supernatural beings born from promises and oaths
