## Campaign Management

### Using These Pages

**Party Knowledge Tracker:**
- Review before each session to recall what players know
- Update immediately after sessions with new revelations
- Use to identify when information should be revealed
- Reference when NPCs respond to party questions

**Conspiracy Tracker:**
- Update after major conspiracy actions
- Track what villains learn through Lyrique's reports
- Plan antagonist responses to party progress
- Identify when conspirators should escalate

**Poison Scale Tribe:**
- Review before the Harrow Keep assault
- Adjust encounter difficulty based on party approach
- Use named NPCs to add personality to combat
- Remember Tik-Tik can provide information if captured

**Plot Threads:**
- Review periodically for inspiration
- Develop concepts further before introduction
- Time revelations for maximum dramatic impact
- Ensure plot threads connect to player character motivations
